Volunteer to Inspire the Youth


In an era, where computer science grows day and night , its really hard to keep the pace with it. And its really hard to learn so much thing in such a short time. But sharing ideas is what helps the most. I guess I first got introduced to teaching through volunteering at code.org. Its a program designed to encourage youth into computer science. And the best part is anyone can become a volunteer, whether you are a basket ball player or a maths teacher or a high school student. I was asked by some teachers to volunteer and I worked with students closely on online, where I introduced them to programming languages starting with the basic. Apart from that I also encouraged them to work on the Code.org site puzzles and other stuffs to get much more passionate about coding. And it was fun. I learnt a lot of new stuffs while teaching. I learnt about working on minecraft projects and a little bit of C#. Overall it was exciting and fun.
Thank you Code.org for taking this initiative.
