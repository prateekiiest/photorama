Managing a TEDx event is hard !

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local,self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program , but individual TEDx events are self-organized (subject to certain rules and regulations).

Likewise, I volunteered for this event and was a part of the core team as social media publicity manager where I worked on engaging more people to this event by sharing important information regarding this among the mass. The event was a brilliant hit, being the first ever TEDx event in any engineering college in the city of joy. And to be a part of it was like a blessing. I worked closely with a lovely supporting team who helped me manage the event , distribute event details among the audiences and reaching out to the TEDx speakers.

I look forward to working with this team again in future for another TEDx event coming this year.
